Testimonium Perhibere Veritati

Sacred Heart College Eziukwu Aba (SAHARCO) is a missionary Catholic boys' secondary school established in 1957 by the Catholic Diocese (then under Most Rev. Joseph B. Whelan, C.S.Sp). Located in Aba, Abia State, Eastern Nigeria. Returned to the Catholic Diocese of Aba in 2012, it continues its legacy of academic excellence, discipline, moral formation, and all-round development of young men.
